Summary Note

This dashboard integrates 4 data sources: Bill Wise Detail (operations), Purchase Data (vendor garment purchases), Sales Registrar Gujarat (regional sales), and main Sales data (company-wide). Caveat: Margins shown are gross (Sales − Purchase) and exclude operating expenses, salaries, transport, and other overheads. For accurate net profitability, cross-reference with final accounts.
